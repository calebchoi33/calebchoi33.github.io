Frequently Asked Questions

If something was to happen to me what would I do?
You will need to order a new one from the website.
How much does it cost to manufacture?
11 USD.
Where do I get one? How does it work?
You can order one on this website. The Con-X wristband utilizes batteries and stores information about the wearer's previously visited locations.
Are other people able to view who I have had contact with?
Yes, people should be able to view in order to recognize who they may have had contact with and who actually has the virus. In order to ensure no person’s ID is known to anyone else, the ID can be starred except the last 3 digits. Additionally, a color coded map of different people’s ID’s will allow those in the area to know who has been infected, who has been in contact with someone and who has no exposure.
Will my private information be shared with anyone?
No, the only way to access your private information is to log into your account via the website with the code on the wristband. In the case of an emergency, authorities will use this tag to call emergency contacts for their authorization instead.
