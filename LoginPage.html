Login Information

Here is where your information would be stored. The only information that won't be hidden is the emergency contacts. In the case of a disaster resulting in you becoming unconsious, authorities will use these contacts to gain authorization to access your hidden information. It will be only with your consent or the consent of your contant that this information be unlocked.

Additionally, if you were to be detected with COVID-19, authorities would be able to access your previous general locations (such as cities or counties) in order to provide extra measures. This would allow the hospital signficantly more convenience in both treating their patients and preventing the flow of more patients.
